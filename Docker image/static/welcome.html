EurOPDX Galaxy


EurOPDX Galaxy instance is dedicated to RNA sequencing and mutation data from PDX models developed by EurOPDX consortium.

Upload your acquired raw sequencing data, process them throught standardized bioinformatics pipelines and check the results in sandbox DataPortal and cBioPortal before making the data set available to the other users.


For more information and step-by-step how-to see: Documentation and Tutorial







About the Galaxy platform


Galaxy is an open platform for supporting data intensive research. Galaxy is developed by The Galaxy Team with the support of many contributors.

The Galaxy Project is supported in part by NHGRI, NSF, The Huck Institutes of the Life Sciences, The Institute for CyberScience at Penn State, and Johns Hopkins University.
